The Story

I’m Jen Bentulan, you may call me Jen, I'm an iOS developer based in Southern California who builds apps from a very personal place. A lot of what I create comes from real life from living with ADHD, dealing with diabetes, and paying attention to the little things that make apps feel either overwhelming or genuinely helpful. That lived experience shapes the way I think about design, flow, and emotional tone, especially in the kinds of apps I’m drawn to build.

I spend a lot of my late nights coding, designing, and figuring out how to make apps feel more human. I’m especially interested in AI-powered experiences, supportive tools, and interfaces that feel warm, thoughtful, and easy to connect with instead of cold or robotic. I care a lot about creating products that are not only functional, but also feel like they understand the person using them.

Outside of coding, I’m into anime, paranormal content, walking, hiking, pickleball, and video games. I like having a mix of creativity, curiosity, and movement in my life, and I think all of that ends up influencing the way I build too.
