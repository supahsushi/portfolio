<h2 class="font-section-header text-section-header text-on-surface mb-6">Redefining Exploration.</h2>

</div>

<div class="md:col-span-8">

<p class="font-lead-text text-lead-text text-on-surface-variant mb-6">

                        Travel can be overwhelming, especially when cognitive load becomes a barrier to spontaneity. WanderBloom was born from the desire to create a travel assistant that doesn't just list attractions, but understands the individual. 

                    </p>

<p class="font-body-base text-body-base text-on-surface-variant">

                        By integrating sophisticated AI personalization with real-time environmental data, the app acts as a digital concierge. It checks the local weather and cross-references it with your personal style to suggest the perfect outfit, ensuring you're comfortable and confident wherever your journey takes you. It’s about removing the friction of planning, allowing the joy of discovery to take center stage.

                    </p>

<h4 class="font-section-header text-2xl text-on-surface uppercase tracking-widest">PHASE 01: CONCEPT &amp; ARCHITECTURE</h4>

<p class="text-lead-text text-on-surface-variant leading-relaxed">With WanderBloom, I wanted to make travel planning feel lighter, prettier, and less stressful. My goal was to build an app that still handled the practical side of travel, like weather, packing, places, and trip timelines, but in a way that felt calm, supportive, and more human. I designed it with an ADHD-friendly mindset and planned the architecture so it could grow into a richer premium experience over time.</p>
